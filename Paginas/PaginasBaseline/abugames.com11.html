Magic Card Filters

Finding the cards you are looking for has never been easier with our detailed filters. They are located on the left or by clicking the filter icon above on mobile.

Actual Picture Items

ABUGames stocks a huge selection of vintage and high end magic cards. For most of these cards we include a detailed picture of the front and back. We call these Actual Picture Items and they are some of the most elite and valuable cards ABUGames has to offer.

View all Actual Picture Items

Graded Magic Cards

If you are looking for investment quality Magic the Gathering cards, collecting graded cards from BGS or PSA is ideal. These cards have had every detail scrutinized and given a rating that confirms their extremely high quality. ABUGames strives to offer the largest selection of graded MTG cards.

View BGS Graded Cards View PSA Graded Cards View All Graded Cards

Misprints

Misprints Magic cards have something out of the ordinary about them. Often they have been miscut, have ink blotches, or have a crimped edge. Misprints are highly collectible due to their unique nature.

View Miscut Cards View Printing Error Cards View Crimped Cards View All Misprint Cards

Artist Signed

These cards were signed by the original artist. Getting artist signed Magic cards is the classic way to "pimp out" your deck.

View all Artist Signed Cards

Dual Lands

The orignal ten dual lands have long been some of magic's most iconic and sought after cards. Allowing you to play mutliple colors with almost no drawback, they are a staple for most vintage, legacy and commander decks. Getting a playset of 40 duals together has long been a must for the ultimate deckbuilders collection.

View Alpha Dual Lands View Beta Dual Lands View Unlimited Dual Lands View Collectors' Edition Dual Lands View Revised Dual Lands View All Dual Lands

Altered

We also have a large selection of cards altered by either the artist of the original card, or a well known artist from within the Magic community.

View Altered Cards

Power 9

The power nine represent the pinacle of Magic collecting. ABUGames regularly keeps in stock a good selection of Black Lotus, Moxes and the Big Blue. Want to impress the ladies? Just say "Hey baby, I've got all five moxes and a Black Lotus".... just make sure they're beta.

View all Power Nine

Artist Proofs

Magic the Gathering artist proof cards are given to artists in very low numbers. They have a regular front, but a white back which makes them not legal for tournament play, but are very collectible.

View Artist Proofs
