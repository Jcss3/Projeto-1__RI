Credit Card Services
We have tried to list as much detail about our store as possible. However, if you can not find information to a question you have, you can always try our FAQ page. If you still can not find it, please contact us.
THE WIZARD'S CUPBOARD has been in business since 1995. First as a physical store then online only, and now as a combination of both at 10525 Park Blvd. in Seminole, Florida. We pride ourselves as being a low cost Magic the Gathering dealer with same-day shipping and personalized service. If you have questions or concerns, please contact us. We make every attempt to answer each and every email within 24 hours.
A SHOPPING CART has been added to our web site for your convenience. We hope you find the pricing information and card search feature useful. We also expect that you will have a lot of fun writing reviews of the cards. Please keep in mind that this is a family site, and we will have to monitor content. If you run into any problems or prefer to contact us about cards, we will be happy to price cards and total with shipping for you.
PRICES ARE FOR M/NM ENGLISH CARDS unless otherwise posted in the product's description! If you are interested in lesser condition or certain foreign cards, please contact us for price and availability. We have added quite a bit to the site but probably not all.
CAN'T FIND A CARD? If you cannot find a card listed, or it is listed without a price, please contact us for availability and price. If an item is out of stock, we may be able to accept an order for it.
QUANTITIES LISTED on the cart are available to the best of our knowledge, but we cannot absolutely guarantee that we have that number of cards in stock.
LAYAWAYS are possible. If you are interested in making a large purchase over time, please contact us.
SHIPMENT will be same day as payment arrives whenever possible. Occasionally we may have to ship next business day. We do not ship on Saturday except if previously agreed upon or on Sunday. We answer the vast majority of our email same day (often within 4 hours) as well.
PAYMENT METHODS accepted are Credit Card (Visa, Mastercard, Discover), Paypal, Bank Check or Money Order, and Cash (at your own risk). Personal Checks will be accepted with the understanding that we will delay shipping for ten days. For all payment methods, amounts are in US Dollars. We do not accept any other currency. Credit card and paypal payments are made subject to our approval. We reserve the right to refund any payment.
CUSTOMER SATISFACTION is extremely important to us. We are rigorous Scrye graders and guarantee card condition to be as stated. It is our sincere desire that you be happy with our web site and any purchase you make from us. If you are satisfied, please tell others. If you have a problem, PLEASE LET US KNOW. Please look at our Grading page to view conditions and how we grade.
  Orders will be held for 14 days awaiting payment, after which time they will be put back in inventory and may not remain available.
  We encourage you to set up an account with us and assure you that we will NOT share your information with anyone for any reason.
  If you cannot find the information you need on the web site, or have an unusual situation you wish to discuss with us, please contact us or call us at (727) 599-0448. Please do not call with intentions to place an order because we can not accept orders through the phone at this time. Thank you.
