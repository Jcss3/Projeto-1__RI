Selling Singles
  1. Go to the MTG Singles/Foils tab at the top of the page.
  2. Select an edition from the dropdown menu or enter a keyword to search the buy list.
  3. Click the "Qty to Sell" button to the right of the card you want to add and then click the number you would like to sell.
  4. Once you have added all of your items, click "Checkout".
  5. Complete the Checkout process as directed.
  6. Read the instructions about how to pack your singles.

How to Pack Singles
  1. Always include your name and order number.
  2. Sort singles in the EXACT order they appear on the invoice.
  3. Remove cards from any sleeves, binders, or other packaging.
  4. Avoid rubber bands, paper clips, and any other containers that may damage your cards.
  5. Cards should be secured in a hard shell case, deck box, fat pack or other container.
  6. Ensure that cards are packed securely and will not shift or fall out during transit.
  7. When packaging your cards do not use excessive tape or anything else that will put your cards at risk by making the package difficult to open.
  8. We encourage you to obtain a tracking number and insurance for your shipment.
  9. Orders must be postmarked within 7 days. Send your order to:

    10. Card Kingdom
    Attn: Grading
    5105 Leary Ave NW
    Seattle WA 98107

    Note: There is no need to include a copy of your receipt, but please DO include your order number.

Receiving Your Payment
  1. Once your order is received we will verify its contents and update its status within 48 hours.
  2. Your cards will then be graded and your order finalized in one to five business days.
  3. Paypal and Store Credit payments are typically issued within 24 hours of the order being finalized.
  4. Checks are issued and mailed the next business day after your order is finalized.


    Card Kingdom is unable to pay for cards not received by our Grading Department, even if they appear on the invoice.
    If a card is not included in your sell order shipment for any reason, we cannot offer cash or store credit.

Selling Bulk
  1. Go to the MTG Bulk tab at the top of the page.
  2. Enter the quantity of each item you would like to sell and click the "Add to Cart" button.
  3. Once you have added all of your items, click "Checkout" on the Shopping Cart.
  4. Complete the checkout process as directed.

Selling Collections
  1. Please contact our Purchasing Department at buyer@cardkingdom.com to take the first step in selling collections, high value foil or non-english cards, or other Magic collectibles. For more information, see our Sell Your Collection page.

Selling Sealed Products
  1. Go to the MTG Sealed/Sets tab at the top of the page.
  2. Select an Edition from the dropdown menu or enter a Product Type to search the buy list.
  3. Click the "Add to Cart" button next to the items you are selling.
  4. Once you have added all of your items, click "Checkout".
  5. If you would like us to contact you before finalizing your sell order, please leave a comment during step #3 of the checkout process.
  6. Complete the Checkout process as directed.

    7. Please Note: Due to Wizards of the Coast policy, we cannot purchase sealed product until 2 years after it's release date.

Grading Guide

Near Mint (NM): Cards graded at Near Mint have little to no damage. These cards still have their sheen. Minor corner whitening or surface clouding may be allowed if the damage is only seen under very close inspection. Excellent (EX): Cards graded at Excellent have minor wear, often from gentle play. Cards in this condition may have small amounts of whitening or border wear, light scratches, or small indents. Mild, widespread surface clouding or sheen disruption also fall under Excellent. Very Good (VG): Cards graded at Very Good have significant wear. Whitening around all edges, moderate surface damage, or larger sections of border wear fall under Very Good. This category also includes more noticeable tactile damage, such as larger indents, shallow bends, or small creases. Good (G): Cards graded at Good are tournament playable, but show extensive wear. This category includes heavy whitening around all edges, heavy surface scuffing or scratches, and large creases. Cards may also show moderate dirt build-up or widespread shallow indents.

Cards showing excessive whitening, inking, creases that can be felt through sleeves, or other extensive damage may fall below our Good category and be returned to you.
