WPN Premium Location

Wizards of the Coast has officially approved the Star City Game Center as one of the first WPN Premium locations in the world!

In addition to the same types of events and promotions that you have come to expect from the Star City Game Center, being a WPN Premium location will soon enable us to further support and grow Southwest Virginia's Magic: The Gathering community in new and exciting ways. This includes hosting exclusive events, access to larger allotments of promotional materials starting with Core Set 2020, and more.

Thank you again for your continued support of the Star City Game Center, and we look forward to seeing you soon!

Games and Supplies

The Star City Game Center stocks an extensive selection of collectable card games, board games, and related supplies. Our on-site staff also has immediate access to the world's largest inventory of Magic: The Gathering card singles and will be happy to help you with whatever you're looking for!

Selling Cards In-Store

Looking to sell or trade your Magic: The Gathering cards? Our buyers are happy to help you and are typically available during the following times:

Wednesday - Friday Noon - 6pm
Saturday 11am - 7pm

If you plan on selling or trading a sizeable quantity of cards, we strongly encourage you to schedule an appointment with one of our buyers beforehand. We cannot guarantee same-day evaluations and/or offers without an appointment.

If no buyer is available, you may leave your cards and contact information with a member of our staff. One of our buyers will usually be able to complete their evaluation and make you an offer for your cards within one business day, but times may occasionally vary depending on the quantity of cards.

In-Store Pickups

Hate waiting in lines? Shop Star City Games's entire website inventory and choose our free "In-Store Pickup" option during the checkout process. Learn more about how you can pick up your order from the Star City Game Center at your convenience!
