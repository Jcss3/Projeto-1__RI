World Championship Theme Deck 1999 #1 Jakub Slemr
 
 
 
 
 
  • Description
These cards are NOT tournament legal Factory Sealed World Championship Theme Deck 1997 World Champion Jakub Slemr came within two matches of becoming the first person to repeat as World Champion.
