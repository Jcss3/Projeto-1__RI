Seasonal Standings

The SCG Tour Seasonal Standings are based on the total number of SCG Points earned during that Season. The Seasonal Standings determine the number of byes a player will receive at individual format Opens and award qualifications for the Players' Championship based on final placement.

Player of the Year Race

The SCG Tour Player of the Year Race is based on the total number of SCG Points earned during Seasons One and Season Two. The Player of the Year Race determines the SCG Tour Player of the Year and awards qualifications for the Players' Championship based on final placement.

At the conclusion of the Season Two Invitational, the player who has earned the most SCG Points that year will be named the SCG Tour Player of the Year.

Earning Byes

During each SCG Tour Season, the Top 16 players in the current Seasonal Standings will receive two (2) byes in any individual format Open they enter. The Top 32 players will receive one (1) bye in any individual format Open they enter.

At the conclusion of each SCG Tour Season, the Top 16 players in the final Seasonal Standings will receive two (2) byes for all individual format Opens during the next season. The Top 32 players will receive one (1) bye for all individual format Opens during the next season.

The SCG Tour Player of the Year receives three (3) byes for all individual format Opens in the next year.

Byes are not cumulative. If a player would receive byes from multiple sources, that player receives the highest number of byes that would be awarded from a single source.

Byes for 2020

The 64 players who have earned the most SCG Points in the 2019 SCG Tour Player of the Year rankings will receive byes in all individual format Opens that take place during the 2020 SCG Tour as follows:

Finish Byes
1st: 3
2nd-32nd: 2
33rd-64th: 1

Points Qualifications for the Invitational

Players who accumulate at least fifteen (15) SCG Points in the current Seasonal Standings prior to the start of that season's Star City Games Invitational will qualify to compete in that event.

Qualifying for the 2019 Players' Championship

At the conclusion of the 2019 Season One Invitational, the Season One Invitational Champion and the Top 3 players in the final 2019 Season One Standings will be awarded invitations to compete in the 2019 Star City Games Players' Championship.

At the conclusion of the Season Two Invitational, the Season Two Invitational Champion, the Top 3 players in the final 2019 Season Two Standings, and the Top 7 players in the final 2019 Player of the Year Race will be awarded invitations to compete in the 2019 Players' Championship.

In the case that a player has already received an invitation to the 2019 Players' Championship, the invitation will instead be awarded to the highest ranked player in the final 2019 Player of the Year Race who has not received an invitation.
