Step into the spotlight.

Star City Games® Opens are the main event of most SCG Tour® Weekends. They award at least $20,000 in prizes to the top competitors, and everyone who makes the Top 8 of an Individual Open or Top 4 of a Team Open qualifies to compete in the next Star City Games Invitational!

All 2019 Season One Standard Open or Classic players receive an exclusive Captain's Quarters playmat!*

All 2019 Season One Modern Open or Classic players receive an exclusive Aurora's Light playmat!*

All 2019 Season One Legacy Open or Classic players receive an exclusive Ripped from Reality playmat!*

All Star City Games Open and Classic players also receive our newest set of five tokens featuring some of your favorite Star City Games personalities!*

*While supplies last.

Tournament Structure

Opens are two-day tournaments with the top finishers after nine rounds of Swiss on Saturday advancing to Day 2. After six rounds of Swiss on Sunday, there is a cut to a single-elimination Top 8.

Saturday
Starts: 10am
Entry: $60 per player
Format: Varies
Individual – 9 rounds, Top 64 or 21+ Match Points advance to Day 2
Team Constructed – 9 rounds, Top 24 or 21+ Match Points advance to Day 2
Sunday
Starts: 10am
6 rounds, cut to a single‑elimination Top 8
